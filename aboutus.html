About Us

Who We Are

The Embrace Foundation is a non-profit orginization in Canada. Founded in 2021 by a group of passionate female volunteers who share the dedication of helping female immigrants and future immigrants adjust to living in Canada.

Our Vision

Together we will make new female Canadians healthy, confident, and successful.

Our Mission

The Embrace Foundation aims to assist new female immigrants and future immigrants from diverse backgrounds, connecting them to community.

Our Values

The Embrace Foundation's services are implemented with the following three core values:
  • Respect
  • Integrity
  • Caring

Services

Our services are here to help you live your life to the fullest by offering services for individuals that are interested or preparing to live in Canada.

We offer services and programs through various online programs, such as workshops and seminars. Services are delivered by highly trained volunteers who provide professional support and suggestions. The service we provide includes the following categories:
  • Owning MotherHood
  • Relationship life Support
  • Love Yourself
  • English Corner
  • Recycling in a Nutshell
  • Parenting
